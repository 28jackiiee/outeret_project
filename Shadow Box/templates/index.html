Welcome to Shadowboxing

The rules are simple. All you have to do is guess a random direction, and the AI will also choose a random direction. If the AI guesses wrong, it becomes your turn. If the AI guesses correctly, it goes again. If it guesses right again, you lose.

If it is your turn and you guess the AI's move, you win a point. If it is the AI's turn and it guesses correctly, it wins a point. First one to two total points wins it all.
